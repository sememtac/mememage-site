Verify your download

The one-line installs check the SHA-256 checksum automatically before running. For a direct download, compare it against SHA256SUMS.txt — print the file's hash and match it to the line for your file:

# prints the SHA-256 of the file you downloaded shasum -a 256 Mememage-Provenance-macOS.zip
(Get-FileHash Mememage-Provenance-Windows.exe -Algorithm SHA256).Hash
